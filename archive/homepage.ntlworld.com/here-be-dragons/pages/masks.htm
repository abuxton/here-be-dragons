Masks and Sculptures

Plastilene clay sculpture of a creature character. The final product is intended to work as a larger than life  display bust, mask, or animatronic/prosthetic piece with a full range of facial movements.
 
 
The sculpture as displayed illustrates the work approximately 50% detailed (with the teeth yet to come). The piece will then be moulded, cast (with a flexible skin) and finished. We'll add some photo's of those processes shortly.
 
The final Character - This is the first pull from the mould, with the teeth and mouth palates in place.

The next stage is to gve him a suitable tongue, and complete the internal animatronics to bring all his facial features fully to life.

  
Alien Mask/prosthetic inspired by the Babylon 5 TV series.

This photo shows the piece as a simple pull over mask. The edges seen here are designed to blend into the skin with the use of prosthetic adhesive/make-up.
This was a simple slush latex piece, used a little while ago as part of a "Vampire murder mystery" style of event.

The original piece was commissioned by the Hybrid company some years ago for use in one of it's own special week long  "interactive  mystery" Holidays.

Much of Second Skin's early work was for these type of "real time" special events.
