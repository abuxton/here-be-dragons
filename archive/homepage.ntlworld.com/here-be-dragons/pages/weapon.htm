Weapons

These are a small selection of the prop foam weapons Second Skin has made over the last few years. Some of the items are one off scratch built pieces. The molded items are cast in foam or fiberglass from silicone rubber moulds of original sculptures from the studio.

Foam/latex swords based on Albion and Orias from the television series "Robin of Sherwood". These are safe for both stunt work and full contact combat sports.
Molded foam rubber full contact combat katanas based on the film "Highlander"
Six foot pole arm (designed in "knock down" form to split into two halves for ease of transportation) Molded fiberglass shield with foam and leather edge padding
